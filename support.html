Frequently asked

Is Twine free?

Yes. Planning your wedding with Twine — discovering vendors, your guest list, budget, registry, timeline, and the Vera concierge — is free for couples.

What is Vera?

Vera is Twine's AI wedding concierge. Ask her about timelines, budgets, what to book and when, etiquette, or to draft a vendor inquiry — she uses your wedding details to give specific advice. Please don't share sensitive financial, health, or personal information in chat.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently and immediately removes your wedding details, saved vendors, guest list, registry, budget, and chat history. This cannot be undone.

How is my data handled?

Your planning data is stored securely and is only accessible to you. See our Privacy Policy for details, and our Terms of Use.

I'm a vendor — how do I join?

Vendors can join the founding cohort at twine.wedding. We'll be in touch with onboarding details.
