V.
What's Coming

Features that make
every competitor obsolete.

Twine launches with the core discovery and outreach experience. What follows will systematically dismantle every remaining reason a couple or vendor would choose anyone else.

Wedding Planners on Twine
Wedding planners are listed as a vendor category — couples swipe and find their planner just like a photographer. Twine supplements planners, never replaces them. Their clients show up more informed. Everyone wins.
Share With Your Planner
One tap exports your entire Twine profile — saved vendors, budget allocation, checklist progress, aesthetic preferences — as a beautiful shareable link or PDF. Your planner gets everything they need in seconds.
Vendor Kanban Board
Saved vendors move through stages: Researching → Inquired → Proposal Received → Booked. Visual, satisfying, and nothing like it exists anywhere. Couples currently track this in spreadsheets.
Featured Placements
Vendors appear in the first 10 cards couples see in their area. Priority by category and zip code — exclusive per market, not just whoever pays most globally.
In-App Messaging
Every vendor conversation lives inside Twine — tied to their card and your checklist. Read receipts so you know exactly when they opened your message.
Verified Reviews Only
Couples upload a wedding photo to leave a Twine review. No anonymous reviews. No fake ones. The direct antidote to The Knot's well-documented review problem.
Aesthetic Match AI
Your vibe quiz trains an algorithm that scores every vendor's Instagram content against your style. Modern minimalist couples only see photographers who actually shoot that way.
