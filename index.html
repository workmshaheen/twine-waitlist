III.Common Questions
Do couples pay anything to use Twine? +
No. Twine is completely free for couples — always. This is intentional. It means more couples join, which means more discovery for you. We make money from vendor subscriptions, not from couples.
What's the difference between Twine leads and The Knot leads? +
On The Knot, a couple submits a generic form and it goes to every vendor in the category within their budget — typically 10-15 vendors at once. On Twine, a couple swipes through your actual Instagram portfolio, specifically saves you, and our AI writes them a personalised inquiry just to you. One vendor. Chosen intentionally. The conversion rate is dramatically different.
What does $29/month actually include? +
Everything. Your full vendor profile with live Instagram integration, Google and Yelp review aggregation, your pricing packages, inquiry inbox, analytics dashboard, and couple discovery. No feature tiers, no hidden costs. Featured placements ($149/month) are available separately if you want to appear first in your area.
What happens after I sign up today? +
You receive a confirmation email locking in your founding rate. Within 48 hours we send your payment link to activate your subscription. When Twine launches in 1–2 weeks, your profile goes live and couples in your area start discovering you. The whole process takes about 10 minutes to set up.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in beyond the monthly billing cycle. That said — founding members who cancel lose their rate permanently. If you return later, you pay the standard $79/month. We think the $29 rate is worth keeping.
Does Twine work for all wedding vendor categories? +
Yes. Photography, videography, content creators, florals, venues, catering, DJs and bands, hair and makeup, wedding planners, officiants, wedding cakes, stationery, transportation, and jewellery. Every category couples need, discoverable by swiping.
