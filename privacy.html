Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Scripps Labs LLC ("Twine", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website at twine.wedding, join our waitlist, or use the Twine app.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us: your email address when you join the couple or vendor waitlist; for couples using the app, your wedding details (partner names, date, location, vibe, budget, guest list, registry, and planning activity).
  • Payment information: if you purchase a vendor subscription, payment is processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details — only a customer/subscription reference and status.
  • Usage information: basic technical data (device, app version) needed to operate and improve the service.

How we use your information

  • To operate the waitlist and notify you about launch and early access.
  • To provide the app's features — vendor discovery, planning tools, and the Vera concierge.
  • To send transactional emails (confirmations, account notices) via our email provider, Resend.
  • To process and manage vendor subscriptions via Stripe.

How your information is stored

Your data is stored securely with our infrastructure providers (Supabase for our database, Vercel for hosting). Access is restricted and protected by row-level security so couples can only access their own data.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the service providers needed to run Twine (Supabase, Vercel, Stripe, Resend, and our AI provider Anthropic for the Vera concierge), and only as needed to provide the service or as required by law.

The Vera concierge

When you chat with Vera, your messages and relevant wedding context are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate responses. Do not share sensitive personal, financial, or health information in chat.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information at any time by emailing us. You can unsubscribe from non-transactional emails using the link in those emails.

Children

Twine is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
